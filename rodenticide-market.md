<p><p>Rodenticides are chemicals used to control rodent populations. Anticoagulants Rodenticides work by preventing blood from clotting, leading to internal bleeding and death. Non-anticoagulants Rodenticides, on the other hand, work by disrupting the nervous system or causing dehydration in rodents. Both types of rodenticides are commonly used in agriculture, food storage facilities, and residential areas to control rodent populations and prevent damage to crops and property. It is important to follow safety precautions and guidelines when using rodenticides to prevent harm to humans and non-target animals.</p></p>
