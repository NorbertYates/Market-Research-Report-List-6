<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speed regulator market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and mechanical. Pneumatic speed regulators use compressed air to control the speed of machinery, offering quick response times and precision in various applications. Hydraulic speed regulators use fluid power to manage speed and ensure smooth operation in heavy equipment. Mechanical speed regulators rely on physical components, such as gears and levers, to control speed, providing a simple and reliable solution for various mechanical systems. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
