<p><p>Ballistic plate market consists of different types based on their level of protection. Level Ⅰ plates offer the lowest level of protection, while Level ⅡA provides protection against smaller caliber handguns. Level Ⅱ plates are designed to stop higher velocity handgun rounds. Level ⅢA plates offer protection against higher velocity handgun and some rifle rounds. Level Ⅲ plates provide protection against rifle rounds, and Level Ⅳ plates offer the highest level of protection against armor-piercing rifle rounds. Customers can choose the level of protection based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
