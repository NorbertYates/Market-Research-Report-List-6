<p><strong>The Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive market is divided into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot melt. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, with low VOC emissions and easy cleanup. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding properties but release harmful chemicals into the environment. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and melt when heated, providing quick setting times and strong bonds. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, making it important for consumers to choose the right adhesive based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
