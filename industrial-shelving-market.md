<p><p>Industrial shelving market includes two main types: Open Industrial Shelving and Closed Industrial Shelving. Open industrial shelving typically features wire or metal shelves with no backing, allowing for easy access to stored items from all sides. This type is commonly used in warehouses and storage facilities where items need to be easily visible and accessible. In contrast, Closed Industrial Shelving includes shelves with solid sides and backs, providing more protection and security for stored items. This type is often used in environments where items need to be shielded from dust or other contaminants.</p></p>
