<p><p>Refractories materials are used in high-temperature industrial processes. These materials can be classified into three main types based on their chemical properties: acidic, neutral, and basic. Acidic refractories are resistant to acidic materials and are often used in industries that produce acidic substances. Neutral refractories are versatile and can withstand both acidic and basic environments. Basic refractories are resistant to basic materials and are commonly used in industries that deal with alkaline substances. Each type of refractory material has specific applications based on their chemical composition.</p></p>
