<p><p>The nanosecond laser market is divided into two main types: adjustable pulse width and fixed pulse width lasers. Adjustable pulse width lasers offer flexibility in pulse duration, enabling users to tailor the laser output for various applications, such as precision material processing and medical treatments. In contrast, fixed pulse width lasers deliver a consistent pulse duration, ideal for applications requiring uniform energy delivery, like laser marking and engraving. Both types cater to distinct industrial and medical needs, enhancing their usability.</p></p>
