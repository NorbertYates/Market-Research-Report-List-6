<p><ul><li>Two Pigment E-Ink</li><li>Three Pigment E-Ink</li><li>Four Pigment E-Ink</li><li>Multi-Pigment E-Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Ink Market Types vary based on the number of pigments used in the electronic ink technology. Two Pigment E-Ink displays use black and white pigments to create grayscale images. Three Pigment E-Ink adds a third color, typically red or blue, for limited color display. Four Pigment E-Ink involves adding a fourth color, usually yellow, to enhance the color range. Multi-Pigment E-Ink incorporates several pigments to produce full-color displays with improved image quality and flexibility for various applications.</p></p>
