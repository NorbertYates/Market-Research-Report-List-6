<p><p>The ink and toner market is divided into two main types: dye ink and pigment ink. Dye ink is a liquid-based ink that produces vibrant and colorful prints, making it ideal for printing photographs and images. On the other hand, pigment ink is made up of tiny solid particles suspended in a liquid base, resulting in more durable and long-lasting prints that are resistant to fading and smudging. Both types cater to different printing needs, with dye ink being preferred for high-quality images and pigment ink for documents that require longevity.</p></p>
