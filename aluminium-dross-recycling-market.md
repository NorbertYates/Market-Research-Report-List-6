<p><p>Aluminium dross recycling market involves two main types: primary aluminium dross and secondary aluminium dross. Primary aluminium dross is generated during aluminium production process, while secondary aluminium dross is produced during recycling of aluminium scrap. Primary dross is usually richer in aluminum content, while secondary dross contains impurities from various alloys. Both types are collected, processed, and recycled to recover aluminium metal for reuse, reducing the need for virgin aluminium production and minimizing waste generation in the aluminium industry.</p></p>
