<p><strong>The ADS-B Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ADS-B In</li><li>ADS-B Out</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ADS-B In and ADS-B Out are two market types within the ADS-B technology. ADS-B In refers to the capability of an aircraft to receive and display ADS-B data from other aircraft around it. On the other hand, ADS-B Out is the ability of an aircraft to transmit its own position, speed, and other data to ground stations and other aircraft equipped with ADS-B In. Both markets are crucial for enhancing situational awareness, improving communication, and increasing safety in aviation.</p></p>
