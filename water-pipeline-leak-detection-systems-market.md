<p><ul><li>Positioning Water Leakage Detector Systems</li><li>Non-positioning Water Leakage Detector Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positioning Water Leakage Detector Systems are advanced systems that provide precise location information of the leaks in water pipelines. These systems use sensors and GPS technology to accurately pinpoint the exact location of the leak, making it easier for repair crews to locate and fix the issue quickly.</p><p>Non-positioning Water Leakage Detector Systems, on the other hand, do not provide specific location information of leaks but instead detect abnormal water flow patterns in pipelines. These systems alert operators of potential leaks based on flow rate and pressure changes, allowing for timely maintenance and repair.</p></p>
