<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle brake fluid market consists of different types such as DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 brake fluid is glycol-based and commonly used in most vehicles. DOT 4 fluid has a higher boiling point than DOT 3, making it suitable for high-performance vehicles. DOT 5 is silicone-based, generally used in classic cars and motorcycles. DOT 5.1 is a newer glycol-based fluid with a higher boiling point than DOT 4, providing enhanced performance in extreme conditions. Each type is designed for specific vehicles and driving conditions.</p></p>
