<p><p>The CPAP systems market consists of two main types - Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices and Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices provide a continuous air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep, while Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices automatically adjust the air pressure based on the individual's breathing patterns throughout the night. Both types of devices are used to treat conditions such as sleep apnea by providing a steady flow of air to prevent interruptions in breathing during sleep.</p></p>
