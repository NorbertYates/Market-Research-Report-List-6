<p><p>Industrial Metal Cleaners market can be categorized into two main types - Alkaline Cleaners and Acid Cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are widely used for removing organic contaminants and grease from metal surfaces, while Acid cleaners are effective for removing inorganic contaminants such as rust and scale. Alkaline cleaners are preferred for their ability to penetrate and emulsify oils and greases, while Acid cleaners are known for their ability to dissolve mineral deposits and oxidation. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and functionality of metal equipment in industrial settings.</p></p>
