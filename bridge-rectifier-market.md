<p><p>The bridge rectifier market is categorized into full-bridge and half-bridge rectifiers. Full-bridge rectifiers utilize four diodes to convert AC to DC efficiently, suitable for applications requiring high current and stable output. They dominate the market due to their versatility in power systems and renewable energy applications. Half-bridge rectifiers, using two diodes, are typically more compact and cost-effective, ideal for lower power applications like battery chargers and small electronic devices. Each type serves distinct market needs based on power requirements.</p></p>
