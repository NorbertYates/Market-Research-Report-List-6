<p><p>In-vitro toxicity testing market encompasses various types of testing systems, including static well plate systems and multi-compartmental perfused systems. Static well plate systems involve cells being exposed to test substances in a stationary environment, while multi-compartmental perfused systems mimic the dynamic conditions of a living organism by allowing for continuous flow of media through multiple compartments. Both systems are used to assess the toxicity of chemicals and pharmaceuticals without the need for animal testing, providing crucial data for drug development and safety assessments.</p></p>
