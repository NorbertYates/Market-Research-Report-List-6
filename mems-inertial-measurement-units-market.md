<p><strong>The MEMS Inertial Measurement Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>4DoF</li><li>6DoF</li><li>9DoF</li><li>10DoF</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs) are categorized based on their degrees of freedom (DoF) for motion tracking. </p><p>- **4DoF** typically combines accelerometers and gyroscopes for basic tilt and orientation detection. </p><p>- **6DoF** includes 3-axis accelerometers and gyroscopes, enabling full motion tracking in 3D space. </p><p>- **9DoF** adds a magnetometer, allowing for enhanced orientation and heading detection. </p><p>- **10DoF** further integrates additional sensors, enhancing accuracy and environmental interaction. </p><p>- **Other types** encompass specialized configurations for niche applications, such as robotics or autonomous systems.</p></p>
