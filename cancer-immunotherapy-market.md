<p><p>Cancer immunotherapy is a type of treatment that uses the body's immune system to fight cancer. There are four main types of cancer immunotherapy markets: </p><p>1. Monoclonal Antibodies: These are laboratory-made proteins that target specific antigens on cancer cells. </p><p>2. Immune Checkpoint Inhibitors: These drugs help the immune system recognize and attack cancer cells by blocking proteins that prevent immune cells from attacking tumors. </p><p>3. Immune System Modulators: These drugs help boost the overall immune response to fight cancer. </p><p>4. Cancer Vaccines: These vaccines help the immune system recognize and attack cancer cells as if they were a pathogen.</p></p>
