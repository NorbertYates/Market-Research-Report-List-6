<p><p>Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) LPE epitaxial wafers are categorized into N-type and P-type layers, reflecting different doping characteristics. N-type layers are doped with elements like Silicon, allowing excess electrons, which enhance conductivity essential for high-speed electronic devices. Conversely, P-type layers utilize elements like Zinc, creating "holes" that facilitate positive charge movement. Together, these layers enable efficient semiconductor device fabrication, including transistors and lasers, crucial for telecommunications and photovoltaic applications, driving their distinct market demands and technological advancements.</p></p>
