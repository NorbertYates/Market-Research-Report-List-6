<p><p>Nano-positioning systems are categorized into continuous control and point to point control markets. In continuous control, the system provides precise and real-time adjustments in position, allowing for smooth and uninterrupted movements. This is useful in applications where constant positioning is required, such as in scanning electron microscopy. On the other hand, point to point control allows for precise movements between specific predetermined points, making it suitable for tasks like semiconductor manufacturing where accuracy and repeatability are crucial.</p></p>
