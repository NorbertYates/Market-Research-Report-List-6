<p><p>The Automated 3D Printing market types include Material Handling, Automated Production, Part Handling, Post-Processing, and Multiprocessing. Material Handling involves automated systems to handle raw materials in the production process. Automated Production refers to the use of robots to automate the entire 3D printing process. Part Handling involves automated systems for moving finished parts within the 3D printer. Post-Processing includes automation for finishing and cleaning printed parts. Multiprocessing market involves systems that can switch between different manufacturing processes within the same machine.</p></p>
