<p><p>Calcined Anthracite is a type of carbon material that is produced through the calcination of raw anthracite coal. There are two main types of Calcined Anthracite markets: Electrically Calcined Anthracite and Gas Calcined Anthracite. Electrically Calcined Anthracite is produced using electrically heated furnaces, resulting in high carbon content and low impurities. Gas Calcined Anthracite, on the other hand, is produced using gas-fired furnaces. Both types are widely used in various industries such as steel manufacturing, foundries, and electrodes production.</p></p>
