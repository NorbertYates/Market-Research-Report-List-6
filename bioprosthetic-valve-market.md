Technological innovations, including improved durability and performance of bioprosthetic materials, are also enhancing patient outcomes and expanding therapeutic options. Furthermore, the increasing healthcare expenditure and awareness regarding cardiovascular diseases are propelling market growth. <p><ul><li>Tissue Aortic Valve</li><li>Tissue Mitral Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioprosthetic valve market is primarily segmented into tissue aortic valves and tissue mitral valves. Tissue aortic valves are designed to replace the aortic valve, often made from animal tissues, offering benefits like reduced risk of clot formation and improved hemodynamics. Tissue mitral valves replace the mitral valve, facilitating proper blood flow between the heart's chambers. Both types focus on providing durable, biocompatible solutions for patients with valve disorders, enhancing quality of life and reducing the need for long-term anticoagulation therapy.</p></p>
