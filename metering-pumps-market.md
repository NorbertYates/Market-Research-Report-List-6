<p><p>Metering pumps are used in various industries for precise and controlled fluid delivery. Diaphragm pumps operate by flexing a diaphragm to create suction and discharge fluid, suitable for low flow and high pressure applications. Piston/plunger pumps use a reciprocating piston or plunger to move fluid, ideal for high flow and moderate pressure tasks. Both types ensure accurate dosing of chemicals, additives, or other substances, making them essential in industries such as water treatment, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
