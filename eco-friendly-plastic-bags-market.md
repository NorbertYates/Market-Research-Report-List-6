<p><p>Eco-friendly plastic bags are available in various market types such as Polylactic acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA is a biodegradable and compostable material made from renewable resources such as corn or sugarcane. PHA is a biopolymer produced by bacteria and is fully biodegradable. Starch blends are made from a mixture of starch and other biodegradable materials, offering a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic bags. These eco-friendly options aim to reduce the environmental impact of single-use plastic bags.</p></p>
