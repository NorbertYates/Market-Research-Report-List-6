<p><strong>The Truck Bedliners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop-In Bedliners</li><li>Spray-On Bedliners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck bedliners are protective coatings used to shield a truck bed from damage. Drop-in bedliners are pre-formed plastic or rubber liners that can be easily inserted and removed from the truck bed. Spray-on bedliners, on the other hand, are liquid coatings that are applied directly onto the truck bed and then dry to form a durable protective layer. Drop-in bedliners are popular for their convenience while spray-on bedliners are known for their customization and seamless coverage.</p></p>
