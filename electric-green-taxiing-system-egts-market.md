External Systems Type involves using separate equipment or vehicles to assist in taxiing, such as tug vehicles or external motor systems. Both types aim to reduce emissions and fuel consumption during taxiing, offering a more environmentally-friendly taxiing solution for airports and airlines.</p></p>
