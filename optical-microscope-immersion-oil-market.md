<p><p>The optical microscope immersion oil market is divided into two main types: synthetic and natural immersion oils. Synthetic immersion oils are engineered materials that offer consistent refractive indices, minimizing light scattering and enhancing image clarity. They are often preferred for their durability and stability. In contrast, natural immersion oils are derived from organic sources and may provide unique optical properties, but can be less consistent. Both types are essential for improving the resolution and quality of microscopic images in various applications.</p></p>
