<p><p>Static mixers are devices used to mix substances in a stationary state without the need for any moving parts. There are three main types of static mixers in the market: channel mixers, pipe mixers, and gas dispersion systems. Channel mixers utilize channels or baffles to create turbulent flow and mix substances efficiently. Pipe mixers use inserted elements within a pipe to blend substances evenly. Gas dispersion systems are designed to disperse gases into liquids effectively. Each type is designed for specific mixing applications.</p></p>
