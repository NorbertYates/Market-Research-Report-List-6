<p><strong>The Steam Turbine MRO Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial</li><li>Radial</li><li>Mixed</li><li>Tangential or Helical</li><li>Reentry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steam turbine MRO market can be classified into various types based on the flow direction of the steam through the turbine blades. Axial turbines have steam flowing parallel to the axis of rotation, radial turbines have steam flowing perpendicular to the axis, mixed turbines have a combination of both axial and radial flow, tangential turbines have steam flowing in a tangent direction, and helical turbines have a spiral flow path. Reentry turbines involve steam being redirected back into the turbine rotor multiple times, increasing efficiency.</p></p>
