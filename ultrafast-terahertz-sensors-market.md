<p><p>Ultrafast terahertz sensors are advanced devices that utilize terahertz radiation for high-speed sensing applications. The market is categorized into two primary types: photonic sensing and electronic sensing. Photonic sensing leverages optical properties and photonic components to achieve high temporal resolution, making it ideal for imaging and spectroscopy. In contrast, electronic sensing relies on electrical components and circuits to detect terahertz signals, providing advantages in integration and scalability for electronic applications. Both types are integral to various fields, including telecommunications, biomedical imaging, and material characterization.</p></p>
