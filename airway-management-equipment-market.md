<p><p>Airway management equipment market includes various types of devices such as endotracheal tubes, laryngeal mask airway, and others. Endotracheal tubes are used to open the airway and allow ventilation directly into the lungs. Laryngeal mask airway is a supraglottic airway device that covers the larynx and allows ventilation without passing through the vocal cords. Other devices in this market include tracheostomy tubes, bronchial blockers, and oral airways, which are used for airway management in different clinical scenarios.</p></p>
