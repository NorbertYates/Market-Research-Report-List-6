<p><p>Pressure sensitive adhesive tapes are available in various types, including PE (polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), PP (polypropylene), PET (polyethylene terephthalate), and other specialty materials. PE tapes are known for their flexibility and conformability, while PVC tapes offer durability and weather resistance. PP tapes are valued for their strength and tear resistance, while PET tapes are known for their chemical and moisture resistance. Other specialty tapes cater to specific applications such as high temperature resistance or clean removal. Each type offers unique characteristics to suit different packaging and sealing needs.</p></p>
