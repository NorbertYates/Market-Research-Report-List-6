<p><p>Worm gear reducers come in three main market types: Orthogonal, Parallel-shaft, and Coaxial. Orthogonal gear reducers are commonly used in applications where shafts are at right angles to each other. Parallel-shaft gear reducers are suitable for applications where shafts are parallel and close together. Coaxial gear reducers have input and output shafts that are on the same axis. Each market type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application in terms of space, efficiency, and torque transmission.</p></p>
