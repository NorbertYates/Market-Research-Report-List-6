<p><p>E-commerce payment market types include real-time bank transfers, offline bank transfers, cash on delivery, direct debits, e-invoices, e-wallets, post-pay, pre-pay, pre-paid cards, and others. Real-time bank transfers allow immediate funds transfer between accounts. Offline bank transfers involve manually transferring funds at a bank. Cash on delivery allows customers to pay in cash upon receiving their items. Direct debits enable automatic payments from a bank account. E-invoices are electronic invoices sent to customers. E-wallets store payment information securely online. Post-pay allows customers to pay after receiving goods. Pre-pay involves paying in advance. Pre-paid cards are loaded with funds before use. Other payment methods may include cryptocurrencies or mobile payments.</p></p>
