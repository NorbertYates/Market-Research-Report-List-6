<p><ul><li>Regular Active Dry Yeast</li><li>Instant Yeast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baking yeast market primarily features two main types: regular active dry yeast and instant yeast. Regular active dry yeast requires proofing in warm water before use, activating the microorganisms for fermentation. In contrast, instant yeast can be mixed directly into dry ingredients without prior activation, making it more convenient for bakers. Both types cater to different baking needs, with regular active dry yeast often preferred for traditional recipes, while instant yeast is favored for its ease and faster rise times.</p></p>
