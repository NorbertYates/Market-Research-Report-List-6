<p><p>Injection stretch blow molding machines are used in manufacturing plastic bottles, containers, and jars. The market is divided into two main types: Single-stage and two-stage machines. Single-stage machines perform both injection molding of the preform and stretch blow molding in one continuous process, resulting in faster cycle times. Two-stage machines separate the injection molding of the preform from the stretch blow molding process, allowing for more flexibility in production and the ability to produce a wider range of container designs.</p></p>
