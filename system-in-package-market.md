<p><p>System-in-Package (SiP) market comprises of three main types: 2D IC, 2.5D IC, and 3D IC. </p><p>2D IC involves traditional packaging where all components are placed on a single layer. </p><p>2.5D IC involves stacking multiple dies on top of each other using interposer technology for improved performance and space efficiency. </p><p>3D IC involves stacking multiple dies on top of each other without an interposer, offering even higher performance benefits. Each type offers its own advantages and is used in various applications depending on the requirements of the system.</p></p>
