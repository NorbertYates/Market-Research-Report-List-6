<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platinum RTD sensors market is primarily divided into two types: wire-wound RTDs and thin film RTDs. Wire-wound RTDs consist of a thin wire of platinum coiled around a ceramic or glass core, providing high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for precision applications. In contrast, thin film RTDs are created by depositing a thin layer of platinum onto a ceramic substrate, allowing for compact design and rapid response times. Both types are widely used for industrial temperature measurement and control.</p></p>
