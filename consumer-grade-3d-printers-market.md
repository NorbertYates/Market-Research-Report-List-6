<p><p>Consumer Grade 3D Printers market can be divided into two main types based on the technology used: FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) and SLA (Stereolithography). FDM printers create objects by melting plastic filament and depositing it layer by layer, making them popular for home use due to their affordability and ease of use. On the other hand, SLA printers use a laser to solidify liquid resin, producing highly detailed and precise objects, making them ideal for professional applications in industries like engineering and design.</p></p>
