<p><p>Water turbines are devices used for converting the flow of water into mechanical energy. There are two main types of water turbines in the market: impulse water turbines and reactive water turbines. Impulse turbines are driven by the impulse force of water hitting the turbine blades, while reactive turbines are driven by the reaction force of water flowing over or through the turbine rotor. Both types of turbines play a crucial role in generating power from water sources such as rivers, dams, and other water bodies.</p></p>
