<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal packaging coatings are divided into two main types: liquid coatings and powder coatings. Liquid coatings are typically applied using a spray gun, roller, or brush, and are the most common type of coating for metal packaging. They provide a smooth finish and good adhesion to the substrate. On the other hand, powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then heated to form a tough, durable finish. They are known for their environmental benefits, as they produce no volatile organic compounds during application. Each type of coating offers unique properties and advantages for metal packaging applications.</p></p>
