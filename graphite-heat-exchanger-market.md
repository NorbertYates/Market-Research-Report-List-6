<p><p>Graphite heat exchangers are primarily available in three market types: Shell and Tubes, Blocks, and Plates. Shell and Tubes heat exchangers consist of a shell containing multiple tubes through which the fluid flows. Blocks heat exchangers have multiple graphite blocks stacked together to exchange heat between fluids. Plates heat exchangers use multiple flat graphite plates to transfer heat. Each market type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different industrial requirements for efficient heat transfer processes.</p></p>
