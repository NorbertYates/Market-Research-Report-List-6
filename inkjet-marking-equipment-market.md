<p><p>Inkjet marking equipment market consists of two main types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-on-Demand (DOD). CIJ systems are known for their ability to continuously print high-quality marks on a wide range of surfaces at high speeds. DOD systems, on the other hand, only produce marks when needed, making them more efficient in terms of ink usage. Both types of inkjet marking equipment are widely used in various industries for product coding, packaging, and labeling applications.</p></p>
