<p><p>The Diesel Fuel Injection Systems Market comprises various types such as the Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System, Common Rail Injection System, and Other Injection Systems. The Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System operates by sending high-pressure fuel directly into each cylinder. The Common Rail Injection System uses a high-pressure fuel rail to deliver precise amounts of fuel to each injector. Other Injection Systems include unit injectors and rotary pumps, offering different methods of delivering fuel into the combustion chamber of diesel engines.</p></p>
