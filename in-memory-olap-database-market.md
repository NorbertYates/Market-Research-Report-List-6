MOLAP (Multidimensional OLAP) stores data in multidimensional arrays, allowing for fast processing of complex analytical queries. DOLAP (Desktop OLAP) is a lightweight version designed for individual use on desktops or small-scale applications. SOLAP (Spatial OLAP) incorporates geographic data into analytics for location-based insights. Other types of in-memory OLAP databases cater to specific needs such as real-time analytics, graph analytics, or industry-specific applications. Each type offers unique features and benefits for different use cases.</p></p>
