<p><p>Shortwave infrared (SWIR) cameras are classified into two main types: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. SWIR area cameras are used for capturing images in two dimensions, allowing for detailed visualization of objects within the SWIR spectrum. On the other hand, SWIR linear cameras capture images in a single line, making them ideal for applications such as spectroscopy and inspection of moving objects. Both types of SWIR cameras offer unique capabilities for various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
