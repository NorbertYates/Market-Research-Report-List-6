<p><p>Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) is a type of dialysis therapy used in critically ill patients with acute kidney injury. It is a slow and continuous form of hemodialysis that helps remove waste products and excess fluid from the blood. CRRT is often used in patients who are hemodynamically unstable and cannot tolerate traditional intermittent hemodialysis.</p><p>The Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy Market is experiencing steady growth, with a projected CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the rising prevalence of acute kidney injury, increasing number of critically ill patients, and advancements in CRRT technology. Additionally, the adoption of CRRT in intensive care units and the growing awareness about the benefits of continuous dialysis therapy are driving the market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy Market include the development of portable CRRT machines, increasing use of citrate anticoagulation to reduce the risk of bleeding, and the integration of technology for remote monitoring and real-time data analysis. These trends are expected to further drive the growth of the CRRT market in the coming years.</p></p>
