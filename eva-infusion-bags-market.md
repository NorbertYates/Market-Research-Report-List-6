<p><p>EVA infusion bags are available in two main types in the market: single chamber and multi chamber. Single chamber infusion bags consist of a single compartment for storing and delivering medication or fluids. On the other hand, multi chamber infusion bags have two or more compartments to store different drugs separately until they are mixed and delivered to the patient. Both types offer flexibility and efficiency in administering medications intravenously, catering to different clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
