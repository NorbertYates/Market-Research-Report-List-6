<p><p>Beamsplitter coatings are used in various industries like medical, defense, research, and telecommunications. Non-polarizing beamsplitters divide light rays without altering their polarization state, making them ideal for applications where polarization preservation is essential. On the other hand, polarizing beamsplitters divide light based on its polarization state, making them suitable for applications where separating polarized light is necessary. Both types of beamsplitters play a crucial role in manipulating light for a wide range of technological purposes.</p></p>
