<p><p>Current sense amplifiers are used in various applications such as high-side sensing and low-side sensing. High-side sensing involves measuring the current on the high side of the load, allowing for accurate monitoring of current consumption and overcurrent protection. On the other hand, low-side sensing involves measuring the current on the low side of the load, providing a cost-effective solution for current sensing applications. Both types of current sense amplifiers play a crucial role in ensuring efficient power management and protection in electronic systems.</p></p>
