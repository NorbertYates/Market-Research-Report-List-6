<p><p>Laser welding systems are classified into two main types based on the type of laser used: solid-state laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. Solid-state laser welding machines utilize solid-state laser technology to deliver high energy pulses for precise and efficient welding. On the other hand, CO2 laser welding machines use carbon dioxide gas as the laser medium to produce continuous laser beams for various welding applications. Both types of laser welding systems are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for their high precision and productivity.</p></p>
