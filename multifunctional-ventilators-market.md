<p><p>The multifunctional ventilators market comprises two primary types: intensive care ventilators and portable/transportable ventilators. These ventilators often include features for high-frequency ventilation and complex adjustments. Portable/transportable ventilators, on the other hand, are lightweight and designed for use in emergency situations or during patient transport.
