<p><ul><li>Welded Steel Grating</li><li>Pressure Locked Grating</li><li>Riveted Grating</li><li>Aluminum Plank Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal gratings market can be classified into different types based on their construction methods. Welded steel grating is made by welding steel bars together in a grid pattern. Pressure locked grating is manufactured by permanently locking cross bars into pre-existing recesses in bearing bars. Riveted grating is assembled by riveting bars together. Aluminum plank grating is made from aluminum planks that are interlocked together. Each type of grating has its own unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
