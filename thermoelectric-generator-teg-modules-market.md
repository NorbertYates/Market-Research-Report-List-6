Multi-Stage TEG modules use multiple stages to generate electricity from a temperature gradient, providing higher efficiency. Single-Stage TEG modules use a single stage to convert heat into electricity, simpler design compared to multi-stage modules. Thermocyclers market involves TEG modules that utilize temperature fluctuations to generate power, suitable for applications where temperature variations are frequent. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
