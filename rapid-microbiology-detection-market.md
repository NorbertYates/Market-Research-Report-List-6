<p><p>Rapid microbiology detection market includes various types such as growth-based, viability-based, cellular component-based, nucleic acid-based, and others. Growth-based methods rely on monitoring microbial growth in the presence of specific nutrients. Viability-based methods detect living microorganisms through metabolic activity or membrane integrity. Cellular component-based methods target specific cellular components like proteins or lipids. Nucleic acid-based methods detect microbial DNA or RNA. Other methods may include biosensors, imaging techniques, or spectroscopy for rapid and accurate microbiology detection.</p></p>
