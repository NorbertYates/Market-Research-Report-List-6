<p><p>The Optical Data Communication market encompasses several key types. Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) enhances bandwidth by transmitting multiple wavelengths simultaneously over fiber. Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET) and Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH) are standards for high-speed data transmission, ensuring synchronization across networks. Fiber Channel is a high-speed network technology primarily for storage networking. Additionally, other market segments include passive optical networks and optical transport networks, catering to various applications in telecommunications, data centers, and enterprise networks, facilitating efficient data transfer.</p></p>
