<p><p>Fiber cement market is divided into three main types based on density: low density fiber cement, medium density fiber cement, and high density fiber cement. Low density fiber cement is lightweight and ideal for use in interior applications. Medium density fiber cement offers a balance of strength and weight, making it suitable for both interior and exterior uses. High density fiber cement is the most durable and heavy-duty option, commonly used in high-impact areas where extra strength is needed.</p></p>
