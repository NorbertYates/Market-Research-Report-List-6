<p><p>Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT) market types include Loose-lay LVT, Dry-back LVT, Stone Plastic Composite (SPC), Wood Plastic Composite (WPC), and others. Loose-lay LVT is designed for easy installation without adhesive. Dry-back LVT requires adhesive for installation. SPC features a rigid core for added durability. WPC combines wood and plastic for a waterproof option. Other types may include click-lock or self-adhesive options. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different preferences in the flooring market.</p></p>
