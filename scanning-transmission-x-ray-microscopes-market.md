<p><p>Scanning transmission X-ray microscopes are advanced imaging devices used for high-resolution analysis of samples at the nanoscale level. They are mainly divided into two types - Transmission X-Ray Microscopes and Scanning X-Ray Microscopes. Transmission X-Ray Microscopes operate by transmitting X-rays through a sample, whereas Scanning X-Ray Microscopes utilize a focused beam to scan the surface of the sample. Both types of microscopes play a crucial role in various fields such as material science, biology, and semiconductor research.</p></p>
