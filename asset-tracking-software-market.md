<p><p>Asset Tracking Software can be classified into two main types: Cloud-based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud-based asset tracking software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access data from anywhere with an internet connection. This type of solution offers scalability, flexibility, and ease of implementation. On the other hand, On-Premise asset tracking software is installed and managed on the organization's own servers. This type of solution offers greater control and security over data but may require more resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
