<p><p>Submarine Air-Independent Propulsion (AIP) Systems utilize cutting-edge technologies to enable submarines to operate without the need to surface frequently for oxygen replenishment. There are two main types of AIP systems: Stirling Engine Submarine AIP Systems and Fuel Cell Submarine AIP Systems. Stirling Engine AIP systems use a heat-driven engine to generate power, while Fuel Cell AIP systems convert stored hydrogen into electricity. These systems provide quieter, more stealthy operations for submarines and allow for longer durations underwater without compromising performance.</p></p>
