<p><p>Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS) is a thermoplastic elastomer that combines the properties of rubber and plastic, making it versatile for various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: oil-extended SBS and non-oil-extended SBS. Oil-extended SBS incorporates oil to enhance flexibility and reduce costs, commonly used in asphalt modification and adhesives. Non-oil-extended SBS, on the other hand, retains higher elastic properties without oil, making it ideal for applications requiring superior strength and durability, such as footwear and automotive parts.</p></p>
