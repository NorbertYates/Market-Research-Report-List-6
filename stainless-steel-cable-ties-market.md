<p><ul><li>Roller Ball Style</li><li>Ladder Style</li><li>Releasable Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel cable ties are commonly found in three main market types: roller ball style, ladder style, and releasable style. Roller ball style cable ties are designed with a ball locking mechanism that provides a secure and durable fastening solution. Ladder style cable ties feature evenly spaced teeth along the length of the tie for adjustable bundling. Releasable style cable ties can be easily released and repositioned without damaging the tie, making them ideal for applications that require frequent adjustments or replacements.</p></p>
