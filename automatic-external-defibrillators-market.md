<p><p>There are two main types of Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs) available in the market: Semi-automated and Fully Automated. Semi-automated AEDs require the user to deliver a shock by pressing a button after analyzing the heart rhythm, while Fully Automated AEDs deliver the shock automatically without the need for user intervention. Both types are designed to provide life-saving treatment for sudden cardiac arrest by restoring normal heart rhythm through the delivery of an electric shock.</p></p>
