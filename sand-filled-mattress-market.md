<p><p>Sand filled mattresses are categorized into two main types based on the material used for filling: PP and PET. PP refers to mattresses filled with polypropylene, a durable and lightweight material that provides good support. On the other hand, PET mattresses are filled with polyester which offers a softer and more cushioned feel. Both types cater to different preferences and needs, with PP mattresses being preferred for their firmness and PET mattresses for their comfort. The choice between the two depends on individual comfort and support requirements.</p></p>
