<p><p>Laser gyroscopes are used in various applications such as navigation systems, robotics, and defense. There are two main types of laser gyroscopes: interferometric fiber optic gyroscopes and resonant fiber optic gyroscopes. Interferometric fiber optic gyroscopes use the interference of light waves to measure rotation, while resonant fiber optic gyroscopes use the resonance frequency of light waves. Both types offer high accuracy and reliability in measuring angular velocity and are commonly used in inertial navigation systems for their precision and durability.</p></p>
