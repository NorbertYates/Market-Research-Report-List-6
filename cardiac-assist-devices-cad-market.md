<p><p>Cardiac Assist Devices (CAD) are medical devices used to help the heart pump blood throughout the body. The types of CAD include Ventricular Assist Device (VAD), Total Artificial Heart, and Intra-Aortic Balloon Pump. VADs are implanted to assist either the left or right ventricle, while Total Artificial Hearts replace both ventricles. Intra-Aortic Balloon Pumps are temporary devices inserted into the aorta to help increase blood flow and oxygen delivery to the heart. These devices are crucial in treating heart failure and other cardiac conditions.</p></p>
