<p><p>Lead acid battery market is segmented into VRLA battery, flooded battery, and others types. VRLA batteries, or Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid batteries, are sealed and maintenance-free, used in applications where minimal maintenance is required. Flooded batteries are traditional lead-acid batteries that require periodic maintenance and refilling of electrolyte. Other types in the market include gel batteries and enhanced flooded batteries. Each type caters to different needs and applications, offering varying levels of performance, durability, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
