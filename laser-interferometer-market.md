<p><strong>The Laser Interferometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homodyne Laser Interferometer</li><li>Heterodyne Laser Interferometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Homodyne Laser Interferometer operates by combining two beams of light that are split from the same source and recombined to measure displacement. This type of interferometer is typically used for highly accurate measurements in applications such as nanotechnology and semiconductor manufacturing.</p><p>Heterodyne Laser Interferometer, on the other hand, uses two separate laser sources of different frequencies to create an interference pattern. This type of interferometer is known for its ability to measure both distance and velocity, making it ideal for applications such as vibration analysis and optical coherence tomography.</p></p>
