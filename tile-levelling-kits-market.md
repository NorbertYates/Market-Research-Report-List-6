<p><p>Tile levelling kits are essential tools in the flooring industry, designed to ensure even tile placement. The two primary market types include the wedge system and screw cap systems. The wedge system utilizes a plastic wedge and spacer, creating tension to keep tiles flush during installation. Conversely, screw cap systems feature a threaded cap that tightens against the tile, providing a more adjustable and robust leveling mechanism. Both systems enhance precision, reduce lippage, and streamline the tiling process for professionals and DIY enthusiasts alike.</p></p>
