<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable projectors come in two main types - DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display). DLP projectors use a digital micromirror device to create images by reflecting light, resulting in high contrast and color accuracy. On the other hand, LCD projectors pass light through liquid crystal panels to produce images, offering vibrant colors and sharp image quality. Both types are popular choices for compact and easily transportable projectors used in various applications such as business presentations, home entertainment, and educational purposes.</p></p>
