<p><p>The decanter centrifuge market is categorized into two main types: two-phase and three-phase decanter centrifuges. Two-phase decanter centrifuges are designed to separate solid particles from liquid, effectively clarifying liquids in applications such as wastewater treatment and food processing. Three-phase decanter centrifuges, on the other hand, can separate two liquid phases and solid particles simultaneously, making them ideal for complex mixtures like oil-water-solids in the petrochemical industry. Both types enhance efficiency and sustainability in separation processes across various sectors.</p></p>
