<p><strong>The Fluoropolymer Powder Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Powder Coatings</li><li>Thermosetting Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluoropolymer powder coatings market consists of two main types: thermoplastic powder coatings and thermosetting powder coatings. </p><p>Thermoplastic powder coatings can be repeatedly melted and solidified, allowing for re-molding and reshaping. They are known for their high chemical resistance and durability, making them a popular choice for various industrial applications.</p><p>On the other hand, thermosetting powder coatings undergo a chemical reaction when exposed to heat, forming a cross-linked polymer structure that is highly resistant to solvents and chemicals. They are commonly used for outdoor applications due to their excellent weather resistance.</p></p>
