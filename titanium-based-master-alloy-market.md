<p><ul><li>Primary Titanium-Based Master Alloy</li><li>Secondary Titanium-Based Master Alloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium-based master alloys can be categorized into primary and secondary master alloys based on their production process. Primary titanium-based master alloys are directly produced by mixing pure titanium and other alloying elements, while secondary titanium-based master alloys are produced by remelting scrap titanium alloy materials. Primary master alloys are preferred for their higher purity and better control over alloy composition, while secondary master alloys are more cost-effective and environmentally friendly due to recycling of scrap materials.</p></p>
