Water-Washed kaolin undergoes a wet processing method to remove impurities and enhance purity. Surface-Modified kaolin is chemically treated to improve properties like dispersion, rheology, and compatibility in various applications. Each market type offers unique benefits and is used in a variety of industries such as ceramics, paints, paper, plastics, and more.</p></p>
