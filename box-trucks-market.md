<p><p>Box trucks are categorized into two main types based on their bridge configuration: Single Bridge Box Trucks and Double Bridge Box Trucks. Single Bridge Box Trucks have a single axle in the rear, making them more maneuverable and suitable for urban areas with narrow streets. On the other hand, Double Bridge Box Trucks have two axles in the rear, offering increased stability and higher weight capacity for longer hauls and heavier loads. Each type caters to different market demands and requirements, providing options for various transportation needs.</p></p>
