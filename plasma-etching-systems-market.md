<p><strong>The Plasma Etching Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP)</li><li>Reactive Ion Etching (RIE)</li><li>Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma etching systems are classified into different types based on their technology and applications. Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) systems use inductively coupled radio frequency (RF) power to generate a high-density plasma for etching processes. Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) systems use a combination of reactive gases and RF power to etch materials with high precision. Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE) systems are specialized for etching deep features in materials such as silicon. Other types of plasma etching systems include capacitively coupled plasma (CCP) and magnetically enhanced reactive ion etching (MERIE) systems.</p></p>
