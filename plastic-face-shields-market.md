<p><p>The plastic face shields market is categorized into three main types based on coverage length: half-length, 3/4-length, and full-length shields. Half-length shields cover the forehead to the chin, offering basic protection for lower-risk environments. The 3/4-length shields extend from the forehead to just below the chin, providing enhanced coverage while still allowing for visibility and comfort. Full-length shields offer maximum protection, covering the entire face and neck, ideal for high-risk situations requiring comprehensive safety measures.</p></p>
