<p><strong>The Integrated Circuit Optical Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nonlinear Photoelectric Coupler</li><li>Linear Photoelectric Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Circuit Optical Couplers are devices used to transfer electrical signals optically between two circuits by using light. Nonlinear Photoelectric Couplers are designed to provide nonlinear response to the input light signal, making them suitable for applications like amplification and distortion correction. On the other hand, Linear Photoelectric Couplers offer a linear response to the input light signal, making them ideal for applications like data transmission and isolation. Both types of couplers play a crucial role in enabling efficient and reliable signal transmission in various electronic systems.</p></p>
