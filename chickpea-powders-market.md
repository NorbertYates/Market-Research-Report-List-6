<p><p>Chickpea powder market consists of two main types: Desi and Kabuli. Desi chickpea powder is made from smaller, dark-colored chickpeas and is commonly used in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines. It has a nutty flavor and is known for its high protein and fiber content. On the other hand, Kabuli chickpea powder is derived from larger, light-colored chickpeas and is popular in Mediterranean and Western dishes. It has a mild flavor and a softer texture compared to Desi chickpea powder.</p></p>
