<p><p>Jewelry store POS systems are available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based POS systems store data and software in the cloud, allowing for easy accessibility from any location with an internet connection. On-premises POS systems, on the other hand, store data and software on local servers within the store. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks, with cloud-based systems offering greater flexibility and scalability, while on-premises systems provide more control over data security and customization options.</p></p>
