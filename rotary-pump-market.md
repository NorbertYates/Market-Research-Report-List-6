<p><ul><li>Gear Pump</li><li>Screw Pump</li><li>Moving Vane Pump</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary pumps are classified into different types based on their mechanism of operation. Gear pumps utilize rotating gears to move fluids. Screw pumps use intermeshing screws to push fluid along the screw's axis. Moving vane pumps contain vanes that sweep along the pump's inner surface to create suction and discharge. Other types of rotary pumps include lobe pumps, peristaltic pumps, and progressive cavity pumps. Each type has its unique design and is suitable for specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
