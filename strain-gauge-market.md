<p><p>A strain gauge is a device used to measure the strain or deformation of an object when it is subjected to external forces. It works on the principle that the electrical resistance of a conductor changes when it is stretched or compressed. Strain gauges are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, construction, and manufacturing for applications like stress analysis, structural health monitoring, and material testing.</p><p>The Strain Gauge Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing demand for accurate and precise measurement of strain and deformation in various industries. The growing adoption of automation and robotics in manufacturing processes is also fueling the demand for strain gauges. Technological advancements in strain gauge technology, such as the development of wireless strain gauges and smart sensors, are further contributing to market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the Strain Gauge Market include the integration of strain gauges with Internet of Things (IoT) technology for real-time monitoring and data analytics, and the development of flexible and wearable strain gauges for applications in healthcare and sports.
