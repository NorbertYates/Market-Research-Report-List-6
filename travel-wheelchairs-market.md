<p><p>Travel wheelchairs come in two main types: power and manual. Power wheelchairs are operated by a battery-powered motor, allowing users to move without physical exertion. These wheelchairs are suitable for those with limited upper body strength or mobility. On the other hand, manual wheelchairs require the user to propel themselves by pushing the wheels by hand. These wheelchairs are lighter, more portable, and often preferred by those who are able to self-propel. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of individuals with disabilities.</p></p>
