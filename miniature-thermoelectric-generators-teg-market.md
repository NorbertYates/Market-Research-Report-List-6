<p><p>The Miniature Thermoelectric Generators (TEGs) market comprises two main types: Single Stage Modules and Multistage Modules. Single Stage Modules utilize a single thermoelectric junction, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for low-temperature differences. In contrast, Multistage Modules incorporate multiple thermoelectric junctions to enhance efficiency by leveraging larger temperature gradients. They are suited for applications requiring higher power outputs and performance in varied thermal conditions. The choice between these types depends on the specific energy needs and operating environments.</p></p>
