<p><ul><li>Fourdrinier Paper Machine Systems</li><li>Twin-Wire Paper Machine Systems</li><li>Multi-Ply Paper Machine Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paper machine systems are categorized into Fourdrinier, Twin-Wire, and Multi-Ply systems. Fourdrinier systems use a long horizontal wire mesh to form the paper, commonly used for producing printing papers. Twin-Wire systems utilize two wires to create paper with improved formation and strength, ideal for packaging materials. Multi-Ply systems combine multiple layers of paper to achieve specific properties such as strength and absorbency, commonly used for tissues and towels. Each system caters to different paper product requirements in the market.</p></p>
