<p><p>The gas grill market primarily consists of two types: propane gas grills and natural gas grills. Propane gas grills are portable, using propane tanks, making them ideal for outdoor activities and locations without a gas line. They offer versatility and ease of use. In contrast, natural gas grills are connected directly to a home’s gas line, providing a continuous fuel supply and eliminating the need for tank replacements. Both types cater to different consumer preferences based on convenience and cooking needs.</p></p>
