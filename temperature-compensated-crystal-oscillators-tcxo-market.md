<p><p>Temperature compensated crystal oscillators (TCXO) come in two main market types: SMD Shape and PIN Shape. SMD Shape TCXOs are smaller and suitable for surface-mount applications, while PIN Shape TCXOs are larger and typically used in through-hole mounting. Both types are designed to provide high stability and accuracy over a wide temperature range, making them ideal for applications where precise timing is critical. The choice between SMD Shape and PIN Shape TCXOs typically depends on the specific requirements of the electronic device or system being designed.</p></p>
