<p><p>Silicone roof coatings are classified into two main types: Anti-Corrosion Coatings and Silicone Waterproofing Coatings. </p><p>Anti-Corrosion Coatings are used to protect metal roofs from rust and degradation caused by exposure to corrosive elements such as moisture and chemicals. These coatings provide a protective barrier that extends the lifespan of the roof.</p><p>Silicone Waterproofing Coatings, on the other hand, are designed to seal and protect roofs from water damage. They provide a durable waterproof barrier that prevents leaks and helps to maintain the structural integrity of the roof.</p></p>
