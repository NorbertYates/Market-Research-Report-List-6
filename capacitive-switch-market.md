<p><p>Capacitive switches can be categorized into two main types: general capacitive switch and hybrid capacitive switch. General capacitive switches function based on the change in capacitance when there is a touch or proximity of a conductive object. On the other hand, hybrid capacitive switches combine capacitive touch technology with additional sensor technologies, allowing for more advanced functionalities and features. Both types of capacitive switches are widely used in various industries for their reliability, durability, and sensitivity.</p></p>
