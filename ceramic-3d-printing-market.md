<p><p>Ceramic 3D printing market is mainly divided into three types based on the material used: powder, resin, and others. Powder-based ceramic 3D printing involves using a powdered ceramic material that is fused together layer by layer. Resin-based ceramic 3D printing utilizes a liquid resin that is hardened and cured to create the final ceramic object. Other types of ceramic 3D printing may involve different methods and materials such as clay or glass. Each type has unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
