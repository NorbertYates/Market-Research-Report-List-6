<p><ul><li>Thermopile Sensors</li><li>Photodiode Sensors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser power sensor market is categorized into three main types: thermopile sensors, photodiode sensors, and other sensors. Thermopile sensors measure laser power by detecting temperature changes caused by absorbed light, offering high accuracy and stability. Photodiode sensors, on the other hand, convert light into electrical current, providing rapid response times and sensitivity across various wavelengths. Other sensors encompass specialized technologies, such as pyroelectric and semiconductor sensors, catering to niche applications with specific measurement requirements in laser power monitoring.</p></p>
