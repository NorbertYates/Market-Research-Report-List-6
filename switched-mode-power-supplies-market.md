<p><p>The Switched Mode Power Supply (SMPS) market includes various types of converters that efficiently convert electrical power. DC-DC converters are utilized for voltage regulation in diverse applications. Forward converters provide isolated output with high efficiency, suitable for larger loads. Flyback converters are versatile, offering both isolation and the ability to step up or down voltages efficiently. Self-oscillating flyback converters simplify design by eliminating the need for external oscillators, making them ideal for compact applications. Each type caters to specific market demands and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
